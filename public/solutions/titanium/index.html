SteelTrace for Operators

Solid Like Titanium

SteelTrace is a Software as a Service that you can deploy on your supply chain to capture structured data from manufacturers and automatically verify that data against your requirements.

Out of the tens of thousands of data points captured by your QA/QC department, SteelTrace helps you spot non-conformities instantly instead of after the fact.

Your Benefits

Automated data verification

By digitizing international norms and requirements, control and monitoring processes become automated and instant.

That lets teams shift from compliance by audit to compliance by design.

Early detection of non-conformities

Real-time information and data verification expose non-conformities during production rather than at handover.

That means fewer surprises and less wasted material on faulty products.

Secure and unchangeable data

All data is digitally secured using blockchain technology, helping teams trust the validity of what they receive.

Operators can verify whether a certificate is real and unchanged.

Time savings and efficiency

SteelTrace automates repetitive quality-management tasks inside one structured workflow.

Your team can focus on technical decisions instead of chasing documents.

Key Features

Real-time data from manufacturers

With SteelTrace, quality-related data is captured directly from the mill.

Operators get one central location with a real-time overview of certifications, endorsements, and product status across the supply chain.

Standardized data

SteelTrace uses a standardized workflow and data format when gathering information from manufacturers and suppliers.

That structure improves completeness, coherence, and reuse of information while reducing siloed reporting.

Data visualization

Structured data can be analyzed at a deeper level instead of living in disconnected PDFs.

Operators can visualize key values and use those insights for faster, more confident decisions.
