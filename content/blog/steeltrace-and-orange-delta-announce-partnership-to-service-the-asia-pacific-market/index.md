The current ongoing pandemic crisis has emphasized a further need for the digital transformation of the industry. It has pushed its pace into a higher gear, and the priority has been placed higher on the agenda, which shows the importance of the partnership between SteelTrace and Orange Delta. Together we can help new clients to boost their digital transformation and create more reliability and authenticity in the supply chain, as well as helping them to reach their sustainability goals and enrich their asset integrity while saving costs in the process.

“The partnership with Orange Delta fits in our strategy to enter the Asia Pacific market. With their experience in the maritime and energy industry, they are the ideal partner for us. We look forward to working with Patrick and his team to make SteelTrace grow in the far eastern markets,” said Tom Meulendijks, CEO and Founder of SteelTrace B.V.

“It is a great pleasure for Orange Delta Equipment Pte Ltd, to represent SteelTrace in the fast-growing Asia Pacific market. SteelTrace provides a highly innovative blockchain-enabled software platform, which improves efficiency, transparency and traceability in the supply chain of steel products, from the source till the end product. Our team in Singapore, will be able to advise local customers how SteelTrace can help them to secure the authenticity of the steel products and to automate the review & compilation of material certificates for their projects”, says Patrick Goossens, Business Development Director of Orange Delta Equipment.

## About SteelTrace

SteelTrace is a platform that gathers all quality-related data of steel products in real-time for easy access for the end-user. All test data, digital signature, witness signatures are available and securely verified with blockchain technology. SteelTrace has developed a full suite of software for all the different players in the supply chain, enhancing supply chain provenance and boosting digital transformation.

## About Orange Delta Equipment

Orange Delta Equipment Pte Ltd, located in Singapore, has recently been established by Patrick Goossens and Hugo Heitling, as an extension on existing offerings of Orange Delta Pte Ltd.

Orange Delta Equipment provides business development and sales representation services, focusing on Marine, Offshore and Renewable Energy markets.

For more information, you can either contact Tom Meulendijks, CEO of SteelTrace ([tom@steeltrace.eu](mailto:tom@steeltrace.eu)) or Patrick Goossens, Business Development Director of Orange Delta Equipment ([pgo@orangedelta.sg](mailto:pgo@orangedelta.sg)).
